GET
/api/v1/documents/{company_id}/by-report
List Documents By Report


Return every report for a company with its uploaded documents and a time-limited signed download URL per document.

Documents whose storage upload had failed (no file_path) appear with download_url: null. Reports with zero documents are still included so the frontend can render an empty card. Ad-hoc documents (no report_id) are excluded — see GET /documents/{company_id} for the flat view.
